# Holika Ki Holi 🌈

**Holika Ki Holi** is a Holi-themed interactive website that presents the culture, traditions, and stories behind the festival of Holi through an engaging digital experience.

The project combines **festival information, interactive features, and entertainment elements** to create a complete Holi-themed web platform.

# Features

## Holi Information
A detailed introduction to the festival of Holi, its cultural background, and traditions.

## Prahlad Story Timeline
A structured timeline explaining the story of Prahlad and the events that led to Holika Dahan.

## Holika Dahan
Information about the significance and traditions of Holika Dahan.

## Holi Dishes & Recipes
A collection of traditional foods associated with Holi along with their recipes.

## Holi Wish Cards
Pre-designed Holi greeting cards that can be shared with friends and family.

## AI Wish Generator
A feature that generates personalized Holi wishes.

## Holi Games
Interactive mini-games based on the Holi theme.

## Holi Quizzes
Quiz section containing questions related to Holi traditions and history.

## Holi Gallery
A gallery displaying images related to the festival of Holi.

## Shop Section
A sample shop interface for Holi-related items.

> Currently this section contains frontend UI only.

## Holi Safety Tips
Guidelines and safety tips for celebrating Holi responsibly.

## Meme Section
A section dedicated to Holi-related memes.

## Music Player
An integrated music player with multiple Holi songs and playback controls.

## Multiple UI Modes
The website supports multiple themes including:

- Light Mode  
- Dark Mode  
- Additional custom visual modes
